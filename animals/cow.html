Spawning

Natural generation

Cows spawn in small herds above grass blocks. However, their spawn rate varies per biome. Most cows spawn as adults, but they have a 5% chance of spawning as a calf, or baby cow.

In Bedrock Edition, herds of 2 to 3 cows spawn in grass blocks at a light level of 9 or higher with at least 2 blocks of space above, except in snowy taigas, snowy plains, meadows, mangrove swamps and wooded badlands. Cows commonly spawn individually above grass after world generation.

In Java Edition, herds of 4 cows spawn on grass blocks at a light level of 7 or higher with at least 2 blocks of space above, except in snowy plains, meadows, mangrove swamps and wooded badlands. Cows rarely spawn individually above grass after world generation.

Villages

A cow sometimes spawns inside animal pens and butchers' houses in villages.

Mooshrooms

Shearing a mooshroom turns it into a cow and drops 5 mushrooms corresponding to the type of mooshroom.

Drops

Adult cows drop:

  • 0–2 leather. The maximum amount is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 5 with Looting III.
  • 1–3 raw beef (or steak if killed while on fire). The maximum amount is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 6 with Looting III.
  • 1–3 experience orbs are dropped when killed by a player or tamed wolf.
  • 1–7 experience orbs upon successful breeding.
Killing a calf yields no items nor experience.

Behavior

A cow wanders around aimlessly, mooing and huffing in small herds occasionally. A cow quickly flees for a few seconds when harmed. Additionally, cows tend to:

  • go to lit and grassy places.
  • sometimes look at players within eight horizontal and four vertical blocks for two to four seconds.
  • avoid going to water unless they are following players and always avoid traversing minecart rails and walking off cliffs high enough to cause fall damage.
  • avoid environmental hazards, such as fire, lava, cactus, sweet berry bushes or dripstone, but make no attempts to avoid explosions.
    • cows can step on pressure plates, jump on dripleaves or farmland and climb ladders, stairs, vines and cave vines.


Milking

An adult cow can be milked by using a bucket on it, yielding a milk bucket.
